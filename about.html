Who We Are 关于我们

Guo Li International is a family-owned and operated hauling and excavation company based in Toronto, proudly serving both residential and commercial clients across the GTA. We specialize in material delivery, site excavation, concrete and asphalt removal, debris hauling, pool and pond digging or backfilling, and seasonal services such as snow hauling and surface clearing. 国力国际是一家在多伦多本地运营的家族企业，专注于建筑材料运输与现场挖掘服务，服务范围覆盖整个大多伦多地区。我们为住宅与商业客户提供建材运送、场地挖掘、沥青和水泥拆除、建筑垃圾清运、泳池及水池的挖掘与填埋、以及冬季的清雪运输和地面清理服务。

Our fleet of 20-yard dump trucks and dependable equipment allows us to handle a wide range of jobs — from driveways and landscaping to full-scale site prep and cleanup. Whether you need to excavate a backyard, remove old asphalt, or backfill a swimming pool, we have the right tools and the right people for the job. 我们配备 20 码大型自卸卡车与稳定可靠的施工设备，能够高效承接各种大小工程——从车道翻新、园艺整理，到大型地面清场和场地准备。无论您是需要挖个后院泳池、填一个坑、拆除旧车道，还是清运成堆建筑垃圾，我们都能按需提供专业协助。

We are also known for our reliable delivery of aggregate materials — including gravel, limestone, sand, soil, and decorative stone — in a range of sizes and volumes. Whether you're building a driveway, preparing a landscape, or filling a foundation, we can supply and deliver the right materials, right where you need them. 我们也专注运送各类砂石建材，包括碎石、石灰石、沙子、泥土、装饰用石料等各种规格。无论您是铺设车道、整理园林，还是需要填筑基础，我们都能按时将合适的材料运送到指定位置，服务高效可靠。
